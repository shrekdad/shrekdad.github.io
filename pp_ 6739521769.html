Mistake Journal Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Mistake Journal, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

User-Provided Information: When you use Mistake Journal, you may provide us with information such as:

  • Your name and email address when you sign up or contact us.
  • Mistakes and study materials you enter into the app.

Automatically Collected Information: We may automatically collect certain information including:

  • Usage data like how you interact with our app.
  • Device information like your device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our app and services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use Mistake Journal to optimize our features.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. However, we may share information as follows:

  • With service providers who help us with our business operations.
  • When required by law or to respond to legal processes.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information using a combination of physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Choices

You have several choices regarding your information:

  • You can review and change your personal information by logging into the app.
  • You can opt out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link in those emails.
  • You can request to have your data deleted by contacting us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
